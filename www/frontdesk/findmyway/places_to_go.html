Places to go

Below are the list of attractions near our property.

  • Merlion Park

    Just in front of Jasmine Hotel is Merlion Park where you'll see the statue which is regarded as Singapore's national icon. The Merlion is a mythical creature with the head of a lion and body of a fish.

  • Marina Bay Sands

    Visit Singapore's most iconic complex a short walk or taxi ride away. Complete with a hotel, casino, shops, restaurants and bars, theatre and an ArtScience Museum. The view from the top is a sight not to be missed.

  • Gardens by the Bay

    Spanning 101 hectares, Gardens by the Bay is an integral part of Singapore's vision of creating a City in a Garden. The Gardens comprises three spaces – Bay South, Bay East and Bay Central – and houses over 380 thousand plants. A suspended 128-metre long walkway links 2 of the iconic Supertrees, offering a spectacular view of the Gardens.

  • Chinatown

    Experience the buzz by walking through the streets of Chinatown. From old temples and heritage buildings to eclectic shops and a wide range of eateries, the area is especially lively during the Chinese New Year period, usually in January or February.

  • Little India

    Immerse yourself in vibrancy of Indian cuisine, merchandise and culture. Around Deepavali (Festival of Light usually around October or November), the place is decorated with lights.

  • Orchard Road

    Orchard Road is known as the most famous shopping area in Singapore. Luxury brands and homegrown brands in several shopping complexes ensure you are spoilt for choice.
